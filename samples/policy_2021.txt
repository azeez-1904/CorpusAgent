MAPLETON MUNICIPAL PUBLIC RECORDS POLICY
Open Public Records Act (OPRA) Fee and Appeal Schedule
Version: v1
Effective Date: January 1, 2021

1. PURPOSE
This policy governs how members of the public may request government records
from the Township of Mapleton under the Open Public Records Act (OPRA), and
sets out the fees and appeal procedures in effect for the 2021 calendar year.

2. RECORDS REQUEST PROCEDURE
Requests must be submitted in writing on the official OPRA request form. The
records custodian shall respond to a request within seven (7) business days of
receipt. If a record is in storage or archived, the custodian may extend the
response period by an additional seven (7) business days with written notice.

3. FEES
The following copying fees apply to records produced under this policy:
- Letter-size pages (8.5 x 11): $0.05 per page
- Legal-size pages (8.5 x 14): $0.07 per page
- A standard records processing fee of $25.00 applies to each request that
  requires more than one hour of staff time to fulfill.
- Electronic records delivered by email are provided at no charge.
- A special service charge may apply where a request requires an extraordinary
  expenditure of time or resources, billed at the actual hourly rate of the
  lowest-paid qualified employee.

4. APPEALS
A requestor whose request is denied in whole or in part may file an appeal.
Appeals must be filed within ten (10) business days of the denial. Appeals are
heard by the Township Records Appeal Officer, who shall issue a written
decision within fourteen (14) business days.

5. CONTACT
Office of the Municipal Clerk, 100 Main Street, Mapleton.
