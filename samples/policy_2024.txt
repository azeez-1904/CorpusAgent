MAPLETON MUNICIPAL PUBLIC RECORDS POLICY
Open Public Records Act (OPRA) Fee and Appeal Schedule
Version: v3
Effective Date: April 1, 2024

1. PURPOSE
This policy governs how members of the public may request government records
from the Township of Mapleton under the Open Public Records Act (OPRA), and
sets out the fees and appeal procedures in effect beginning in 2024. This
version supersedes the 2023 policy.

2. RECORDS REQUEST PROCEDURE
Requests must be submitted through the Township's online portal or in writing
on the official OPRA request form. The records custodian shall respond to a
request within five (5) business days of receipt. If a record is archived, the
custodian may extend the response period by an additional seven (7) business
days with written notice.

3. FEES
The following copying fees apply to records produced under this policy:
- Letter-size pages (8.5 x 11): $0.05 per page
- Legal-size pages (8.5 x 14): $0.07 per page
- A standard records processing fee of $40.00 applies to each request that
  requires more than one hour of staff time to fulfill.
- Electronic records delivered by email are provided at no charge.
- A special service charge may apply where a request requires an extraordinary
  expenditure of time or resources.

4. APPEALS
A requestor whose request is denied in whole or in part may file an appeal.
Appeals must be filed within thirty (30) business days of the denial. Appeals
are heard by the Township Records Appeal Officer, who shall issue a written
decision within ten (10) business days. A requestor may alternatively appeal
directly to the Government Records Council.

5. CONTACT
Office of the Municipal Clerk, 100 Main Street, Mapleton.
